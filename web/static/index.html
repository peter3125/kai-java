KAI SEARCH

KAI is an artificial intelligence. KAI reads and analyses your emails, documents, databases, audio and video recordings and whatever else you want analyzed. KAI is a scalable big-data solution containing sophisticated natural language processors, and neural networks. KAI learns from its users, and gives you invaluable insights into your data.

Breathe new life into your data

What do you want to do with your data? Can't remember where you put that document? KAI goes beyond search and can bring your documents or information in those documents to you. KAI can copy documents it has processed to a location of your choice, email you about them, or tell you about them on your personalised KAI dashboard.

Find stuff

Interactively search KAI using simple questions about your data. Search KAI using keywords as you’re already used to. KAI sports a semantic search engine, covering large volumes of data with simple keywords, much more so than ordinary keyword search engines. Semantic search can also be used to zoom in on data that keywords can’t. Looking for an action? A thing, a person? KAI knows the difference, and you can teach it if it doesn’t. KAI also supports complex search operations for the more advanced users. Boolean operators, time slices, and many others.

Information management

Start by knowing what it is you have in your organisation. How many files, what kinds of files? Where are these files located? How large are these files? How old are these files? Then move up to proactively managing your information. What data needs archiving? removal? security updating, or classification? Construct simple rules for how you want data to be managed as it arrives. Have KAI contact you, move files, or just act and log.
